STATION 26 JUICY BANGER

DRAFT - IPA | ABV: 7.4%

Based in Denver, Colorado, Station 26's Juicy Banger IPA is a Sensational IPA, Especially Within This Bar's Community! Station 26 Was Able To Succesfully Combine A Plethora Of Flavor Profiles Including Juicy (no-brainer), Tropical, Fruity, Vibrant, Hoppy, etc. This IPA Primarily Brings Out Flavors of Citrus, Mango, and Other Fruits To Create An Overall Refreshing and Aromatic Experience. This Beer Has Been Known To Appeal To Those Who Appreciate Bitterness and Lush Flavors.

VOODOO RANGER IPA

DRAFT - IPA | ABV: 7%

Although Highly Recognized, Voodoo Ranger IPA is Based Out Of Fort Collins, Colorado, Thanks To New Belgium Brewery! This Craft Beer Is Renowned For Its Bold, Hop-Foward Profile Combined With A Solid Malt Backbone, Creating A Well-Balanced and Highly Drinkable IPA! It Primarily Showcases The Aromatic Qualities of Hops By Highlighting Citrus & Troplical Fruits Such As Grapefruits, Mangoes, and Pineapples. This IPA Is Further Appreciated For Its Ability To Deliver A Robust Hop Character Without Overwhelming The Palate, Overall.

VOODOO RANGER JUICE FORCE IPA

CANNED - IPA | ABV: 9.5%

Although Highly Recognized, Voodoo Ranger IPA is Based Out Of Fort Collins, Colorado, Thanks To New Belgium Brewery! This Craft IPA Is Renowned As A Hazy and Juicy IPA For Its Bold, Fruit-Forward Hop Character and Evidently Higher ABV Content! It Is Described To Have More Of An Explosive Aroma of Tropical Fruit Flavors Such As Mango, Pineapple, and Citrus. It Is Accredited To Have A Fine Balance Of Hoppy, Nectar-Like Quality With A Bitterness Aspect Which Creates A Complex Yet Harmonious Taste Experience! Designed For Those Who Appreciate Hops But Desire A Sweet, Aromatic Punch, Similar To A Tropical Fruit Smoothie
