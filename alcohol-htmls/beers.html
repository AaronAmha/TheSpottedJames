CRAFT BEERS
UPSLOPE CITRA PALE ALE

DRAFT - CPA | ABV: 5.8%

Based in the Great: Boulder, Colorado, Upslope Brewery Has Cultivated Yet Another Elegant Way To Combine A Hoppy & Refreshing Taste Profile By Utilizing Citra Hops. Citra Hops Are Common In The Craft Beer Industry As They Provide A Strong Tropical Citrus/Fruity Flavor To The Beer.

VOODOO RANGER IPA

DRAFT - IPA | ABV: 7%

Although Highly Recognized, Voodoo Ranger IPA is Based Out Of Fort Collins, Colorado, Thanks To New Belgium Brewery! This Craft Beer Is Renowned For Its Bold, Hop-Foward Profile Combined With A Solid Malt Backbone, Creating A Well-Balanced and Highly Drinkable IPA! It Primarily Showcases The Aromatic Qualities of Hops By Highlighting Citrus & Troplical Fruits Such As Grapefruits, Mangoes, and Pineapples. This IPA Is Further Appreciated For Its Ability To Deliver A Robust Hop Character Without Overwhelming The Palate, Overall.

VOODOO RANGER JUICE FORCE IPA

CANNED - IPA | ABV: 9.5%

Although Highly Recognized, Voodoo Ranger IPA is Based Out Of Fort Collins, Colorado, Thanks To New Belgium Brewery! This Craft IPA Is Renowned As A Hazy and Juicy IPA For Its Bold, Fruit-Forward Hop Character and Evidently Higher ABV Content! It Is Described To Have More Of An Explosive Aroma of Tropical Fruit Flavors Such As Mango, Pineapple, and Citrus. It Is Accredited To Have A Fine Balance Of Hoppy, Nectar-Like Quality With A Bitterness Aspect Which Creates A Complex Yet Harmonious Taste Experience! Designed For Those Who Appreciate Hops But Desire A Sweet, Aromatic Punch, Similar To A Tropical Fruit Smoothie

BLUE MOON

DRAFT - CRAFT(EHH) | ABV: 5.4%

Although Highly Recognized As A Multinational Brewery, Blue Moon is Based Out Of Denver, Colorado, By A Brewer At the Coors Field's SandLot Brewery! Therefore, This *Craft* Beer Has Beem Included... Blue Moon Is A Belgian-Style Wheat Ale Renowned For Its Smooth, Refreshing Taste Complimented With An Orange Slice To Enhance Its Citrus Aroma and Flavor. Highly Associated To Taste Profiles Of Creamy and Silky With Hints of Coriander Spice, Yet Malty and Grainy.

STATION 26 JUICY BANGER

DRAFT - IPA | ABV: 7.4%

Based in Denver, Colorado, Station 26's Juicy Banger IPA is a Sensational IPA, Especially Within This Bar's Community! Station 26 Was Able To Succesfully Combine A Plethora Of Flavor Profiles Including Juicy (no-brainer), Tropical, Fruity, Vibrant, Hoppy, etc. This IPA Primarily Brings Out Flavors of Citrus, Mango, and Other Fruits To Create An Overall Refreshing and Aromatic Experience. This Beer Has Been Known To Appeal To Those Who Appreciate Bitterness and Lush Flavors.

OSKAR BLUES' DALE'S PALE ALE

AMERICAN PALE ALE | CANNED | ABV: 5.1%

Based in Longmont, Colorado, Dale's Pale Ale is Oskar Blues Brewery's Signature Pale Ale. This Pale Ale Is Highly Recongized For Challenging The Status Quo That Canned Beer Can't Offer Quality and Complexity! This Craft Beer Is Acknowledged To Have A Robust Hop Profile, Providing Flavors and Aromas of Citrus, Pine, and Floral Notes Combined and Balanced With Foundations of A Solid Malty Profile. The Malty Profile Is Noted To Additionally Provide A Slight Sweetness and Caramel-y Flavor

NEW BELGIUM'S FAT TIRE

AMBER ALE | CANNED | ABV: 5.3%

Based in Fort Collins, Colorado, Fat Tire is One Of New Belgium Brewery's Signature Beers. This Amber Ale Is Acknowledged To Have a Toasty, Biscuit-like Malty Backbone Complemented By A Mild Sweetness. Its Hop Profile Is Rather Subtle, Offering A Slight Floral and Herbal Note That Balances The Malt Without Overpowering The Bitterness.

DRY DOCK'S APRICOT BLONDE

CANNED | ABV: 5.1%

Based in Aurora, Colorado, Apricot Blonde is Dry Dock Brewery's Signature Beers. A Combination Of Fruity Apricot Flavors Combined With The Light & Crisp Taste That Comes With A Blonde Ale Gives This Beer It's Unique Flavor Profile!

ODELL BREWING'S 90 SHILLING ALE

AMBER ALE | CANNED | ABV: 5.3%

Fun Fact: The Term "90 Shilling" Comes From The Scottish Method Of Taxing Beer, As Beers Were Taxed Based On Their Stength, Indicating 90 Shilling To Be A Medium-Bodied Option That Isn't Too Light, Nor Too Strong!

Based in Fort Collins, Colorado, 90 Shilling Ale is One Of Odell Brewery's Signature Beers. This Amber Ale Is The Perfect Balance Between Malt And Hops. It Is Known To Provide A Smooth, Malty Profile With Hints of Caramel Making It Flavorful & It Easy To Drink.
